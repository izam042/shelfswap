Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 2024

1. Shipping Process

At ShelfSwap, we strive to make the shipping process as smooth as possible for both buyers and sellers. Here's how our shipping works:

  • Once an order is placed, sellers have 2 business days to prepare the book for pickup
  • Our logistics partner will pick up the book from the seller's address
  • Books are carefully packaged and shipped to the buyer's address
  • Tracking information is provided for all shipments

2. Shipping Timeframes

  • Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Delivery times may vary based on location and weather conditions
  • Rural areas may experience slightly longer delivery times

3. Tracking Your Order

You can track your order through our website or mobile app. We'll send you regular updates about your shipment's status via email and SMS.

4. International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. We are working on expanding our shipping services to other countries in the future.

5. Lost or Damaged Packages

In the rare event that your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact our customer support immediately. We will work with our logistics partner to resolve the issue.
