Refunds and Cancellations

Last updated: April 2024

1. Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order under the following conditions:

  • Before the seller has shipped the book
  • Within 1 hour of placing the order
  • If the seller fails to ship within 2 business days

To cancel an order, please contact our customer support or use the cancellation option in your order history.

2. Refund Policy

We offer refunds in the following cases:

  • If the book received is significantly different from the description
  • If the book is damaged during shipping
  • If the wrong book is delivered
  • If the order is cancelled before shipping

3. Refund Process

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of receiving the book
  • You may be required to provide photos of the damaged or incorrect book
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Books with minor wear and tear as described in the listing
  • Books that have been written in or highlighted
  • Digital content or access codes
  • Shipping charges (unless the return is due to our error)

5. Return Shipping

If a return is approved:

  • We will provide a prepaid return shipping label
  • Books must be returned in their original condition
  • Returns must be shipped within 7 days of approval
  • Refund will be processed after we receive and verify the returned book
