About ShelfSwap

Revolutionizing how students access academic books in India through a sustainable, student-powered marketplace

Our Mission

At ShelfSwap, we're on a mission to revolutionize how students access academic books in India by creating a sustainable, affordable, and efficient marketplace.

Textbooks are expensive, and most second-hand platforms today act like unstructured classifieds—no payments, no delivery, no guarantees. We're changing that by offering a seamless, Amazon-like experience for used books that's built by students, for students.

What We Do

We're building India's first fully standardized second-hand academic bookstore, where every transaction is smooth, safe, and reliable. ShelfSwap handles everything from payments to delivery, ensuring a hassle-free experience for both buyers and sellers.

Secure Payments

Multiple payment options with secure transaction processing and buyer protection policies

Doorstep Service

Convenient pickup from sellers and delivery to buyers, right at your doorstep

Quality Verification

Thorough inspection and standardized quality grading for all books

Affordable Pricing

Up to 70% off retail prices for buyers and maximum returns for sellers

Our Impact

Beyond logistics, we're creating something bigger: a student-powered education economy that makes learning more accessible while reducing waste.

Every book swapped helps reduce educational waste, makes learning more affordable, and empowers students to support each other's growth. Whether you're clearing out last semester's shelf or preparing for the next, ShelfSwap makes it effortless—and impactful.
